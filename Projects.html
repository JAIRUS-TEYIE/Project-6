Project 1:

Coastal Geomorphology Mapping and shoreline change analysis

The project focused on the study of the physical process that shape the coastal landscape like Coastal erosion, sediment transport, beach dynamics and sea-level dynamics.
Therefore, the main aim was to carry out temporal analysis of the shoreline of coastal areas to determine the change detection for a span of 10years using QGIS software.
